**Fitz and the Tantrums** bring their horn-driven, hook-heavy indie-pop to Chateau Ste. Michelle's outdoor amphitheater on **Sunday, July 26, at 7 p.m.**, on their Man on the Moon Tour, with Dylan Chambers opening. If "HandClap" or "The Walker" have ever gotten stuck in your head, this is a full night of that same danceable, feel-good energy — a solid pick for the pop crowd.

Chateau Ste. Michelle is a genuine wine-country amphitheater experience: a 15-20 minute drive to Woodinville, lawn seating (bring a blanket and low chairs), and a picnic-friendly vibe — you can even bring your own food. It's an easy eastside yes for a Sunday evening show that doesn't run too late.

Tickets and details at [ste-michelle.com](https://www.ste-michelle.com/concerts/summer-concerts).
