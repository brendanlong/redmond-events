**Molly Tuttle** — a Grammy-winning guitarist and songwriter who's redefined what bluegrass and Americana can sound like — plays the Kirkland Performance Center on **Thursday, July 30, at 7 p.m.** Her 2025 album *So Long Little Miss Sunshine* picked up two Grammy nominations, including one for the single "That's Gonna Leave a Mark," and her live show backs up the hype: virtuosic flatpicking married to genuinely sharp songwriting that pulls from bluegrass, pop, and rock in equal measure.

This isn't a bar-show version of Americana — KPC is a proper seated theater, so expect a real listening-room show rather than background music. Tickets run $57-$79.

It's about a 20-minute drive from downtown Redmond out to Kirkland, an easy Thursday-night trip since it's headed away from Seattle traffic rather than into it. Tickets and details at [explorekirkland.com](https://www.explorekirkland.com/events/molly-tuttle/).
