On **Thursday, July 23**, Gearhouse is organizing a casual evening that starts with happy hour at their Capitol Hill shop and ends with a free outdoor concert at Volunteer Park. The plan: gather at Gearhouse around 5 p.m., have a drink and some good company, then walk over to Volunteer Park together at 6:30 p.m. for the evening's live music.

Volunteer Park hosts a free summer concert series in its grounds — it's one of those nice Seattle summer traditions where you bring a blanket, sit on the lawn, and let someone else's playlist handle the evening. The park is right in Capitol Hill, a short walk from the Gearhouse shop, so the logistics are simple.

Honest note on the drive from Redmond: Capitol Hill on a Thursday evening is about a **25–30 minute drive**, and evening traffic on I-5 or SR-520 can add time. This is one where carpooling makes a lot of sense — if a few people are going, make a night of the commute.

The concert itself is free and open to anyone; the happy hour beforehand is the Gearhouse part. RSVP on the [Gearhouse calendar](https://www.joingearhouse.com/) if you're coming for the group portion so they have a headcount.
