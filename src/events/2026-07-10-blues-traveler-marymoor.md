Marymoor's summer concert series opens with a genuine '90s alt-rock triple bill: **Blues Traveler**, **Gin Blossoms**, and **Spin Doctors** share the outdoor stage on **Friday, July 10, at 6 p.m.** If you know "Run-Around," "Hey Jealousy," or "Two Princes," you know all three of these bands without realizing it — this is a nostalgia show built for anyone who came of age on adult-alternative radio.

The best part for us: it's at **Marymoor Park**, a 10-minute walk or five-minute bike ride from downtown Redmond. No parking hassle, no long drive, no bridge traffic — just grab a blanket and walk over for an evening of jangly guitar hits under the stars.

Gates typically open well before the 6 p.m. start; check [marymoorlive.com](https://www.marymoorlive.com/) for the full set schedule and tickets, sold through Ticketmaster. This is the first of several big Marymoor shows this summer — worth keeping an eye on the lineup if this one isn't your speed.
