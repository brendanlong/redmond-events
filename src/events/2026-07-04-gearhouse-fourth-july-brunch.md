If you want to start the Fourth of July outdoors and with good company before things get hectic, Gearhouse is hosting a **beachside brunch at Madrona Park** on the morning of July 4, 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. They're bringing camp stoves and the makings for build-your-own breakfast sandwiches; you bring something to share and an appetite.

Madrona Park sits right on Lake Washington, with a sandy beach, a dock, and the kind of morning light that makes breakfast outside feel like the correct decision. It's in the Madrona neighborhood of Seattle — about a **25-minute drive from downtown Redmond** via I-90, and worth it for a summer holiday morning before the day turns into fireworks logistics.

The format is deliberately low-key: show up, eat, hang out, enjoy the lake. Whether it's a warmup before a full day of July 4th activities or the main event is up to you.

RSVP on the [Gearhouse calendar](https://www.joingearhouse.com/) so they can count on the right amount of food. Bring a blanket or low chair — it's a park, not a picnic table situation — and something shareable for the potluck side of things.
