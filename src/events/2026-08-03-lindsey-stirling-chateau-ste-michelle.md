**Lindsey Stirling** brings her *Duality Untamed* tour to Chateau Ste. Michelle on
Monday, August 3, with **PVRIS** opening — and this one feels tailor-made for the
nerdier end of our music taste. Stirling's whole thing is dancing, dubstep-inflected
violin built around video-game, fantasy, and film-score energy: a genuinely
theatrical show rather than someone standing still with a fiddle.

The setting helps. It's an outdoor evening show on the winery lawn in Woodinville —
about a **15-minute drive north from downtown Redmond** up Redmond–Woodinville Road,
which makes it one of the most painless big-name concerts you'll get all summer (no
trek across the bridges into the city). Bring a blanket and low chairs, get there
early, and picnic on the grass before the lights go down.

Tickets are through Ticketmaster and lawn seats are the move; grab them off the
[Chateau summer concerts page](https://www.ste-michelle.com/concerts/summer-concerts).
A 7 p.m. start on a Monday means it's a school-night show, so plan the drive home
accordingly — but for this lineup, well worth it.
