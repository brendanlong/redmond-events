If you've never wandered by on a Saturday morning, **Exotics at Redmond Town Center** is worth knowing about: it's billed as the largest weekly exotic car gathering in the US, drawing everything from Lamborghinis and vintage Porsches to rare JDM imports into the RTC south lot, every Saturday from **9 to 11 a.m.**, spring through fall. No entry fee, no registration to just look — owners show up, park, and hang out while people wander the rows.

It's an easy one to fold into a downtown Redmond Saturday morning — grab coffee, walk over, and spend twenty minutes looking at cars you'll never own. A few Saturdays get themed specialty shows this summer: **Italian Car Day on July 11** and **German Car Day on August 8** among them, which tend to draw an even denser lineup.

Since it's a standing weekly thing at Redmond Town Center, there's no ticket to buy — just show up on a Saturday morning. Current schedule and specialty-day themes are at [exoticsat.com](https://exoticsat.com/).
