For the pickleball crowd (and there are a lot of you): the City of Redmond is hosting a **competitive doubles pickleball tournament** at Perrigo Park on **Saturday, July 18**, a fast-paced day of matches on the park's dedicated outdoor courts.

If you play, this is a chance to test yourself against other serious players in a real bracket rather than open-play; if you don't, it's still a fun free spectator afternoon — pickleball crowds tend to be friendly and the rallies get genuinely competitive at this level. Perrigo Park is on the north side of Redmond, about a 10-minute drive from downtown with four dedicated courts, restrooms, and plenty of parking.

Details and registration are through the [City of Redmond Parks & Recreation](https://www.redmond.gov/165/Parks-Recreation) office.
