Closing out the summer on a nostalgic note: **The Beach Boys** play Marymoor Park on **Sunday, August 30, at 6:30 p.m.** — "Good Vibrations," "Wouldn't It Be Nice," "California Girls," the whole surf-pop songbook that basically invented the summer-anthem genre everyone else has been chasing since. It's a legacy act, but a genuinely legendary one, and a perfect way to send off the season.

Same walkable Marymoor setup as always: a stroll from downtown Redmond, no parking to think about, and a big, easygoing outdoor crowd for a Sunday-evening show. Bring the family — this is about as broadly crowd-pleasing as a summer concert gets, kids and grandparents included.

Tickets and set times at [marymoorlive.com](https://www.marymoorlive.com/), via Ticketmaster.
