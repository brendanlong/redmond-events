A small, sweet one for anyone with kids (or who just likes farm equipment): **Farrel-McWhirter Farm Park** is hosting **Touch a Tractor** on **Thursday, July 30**, where the farm's big green tractor, "Hulk," comes out for kids to climb on and get a close-up look at real farm machinery. It's free, low-key, and all-ages — no registration needed, just show up.

Farrel-McWhirter is Redmond's working farm park on the north side of town — a genuine barnyard with animals, trails, and picnic shelters, about a 10-minute drive from downtown. Pair it with a walk through the barnyard or one of the park's trails to make a morning of it.

Details at [redmond.gov/288/Farrel-McWhirter-Park](https://www.redmond.gov/288/Farrel-McWhirter-Park).
