**Alison Krauss & Union Station**, featuring dobro virtuoso **Jerry Douglas**, play Marymoor Park on **Saturday, August 22, at 6 p.m.**, with Jerry Douglas and Theo Lawrence also on the bill. If the name doesn't immediately register, the résumé will: Krauss is the most-awarded woman in Grammy history, with a voice and a bluegrass/Americana band that turn even non-bluegrass fans into believers over the course of a set.

This one's outside our usual rock/pop/metal wheelhouse, but it's exactly the kind of "exceptional, can't-miss" pick worth stepping outside your regular lane for — and it's Marymoor, so there's zero extra cost to trying it: a walk from downtown Redmond, no parking to fight, just a lawn chair and a genuinely world-class live band.

Tickets and set times at [marymoorlive.com](https://www.marymoorlive.com/), via Ticketmaster.
