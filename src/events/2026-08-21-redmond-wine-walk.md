The **5th Annual Redmond Wine Walk** returns to Redmond Town Center on **Friday, August 21, 5-8 p.m.**, timed to WAugust — Washington's month-long celebration of local wine. Ticket-holders get a tasting glass and walk between stops around the Town Center sampling pours from wineries across the state. New this year for the anniversary edition: a **Women of Washington Wine Trail**, spotlighting women-led wineries with exclusive tasting stops as part of the walk.

This is about as easy as it gets for our readers — it's happening right in downtown Redmond, walkable for anyone nearby, no driving or parking required. It's a nice, low-key way to spend a Friday evening: sample some wine, wander the Town Center, and not have to think about a ride home.

Advance tickets are **$35** (day-of $45, if still available), 21+ with photo ID required at check-in. Tickets and full details at [redmondtowncenter.com/events](https://redmondtowncenter.com/events/).
