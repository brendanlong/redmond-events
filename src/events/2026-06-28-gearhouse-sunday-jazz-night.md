Gearhouse runs a **Sunday Jazz Night** at its Capitol Hill shop on select Sundays — a live jazz trio (piano, bass, drums) playing while you read, hang out, or just let the Sunday scaries dissolve a little. Music runs 5–7 p.m.; they ask you to arrive by 4:30 to snag a seat. It's a 21+ event, and the vibe is more relaxed coffee-shop listening session than performance venue.

The next two dates are **tonight (June 28)** and **July 19**, both at 5 p.m.

One honest note: this is a Sunday evening in Seattle, and the Gearhouse shop is in Capitol Hill — about a **25-minute drive from Redmond** on I-5 or I-90, depending on which direction you come from. Sunday evening traffic is usually reasonable, but factor in the drive home afterward. That said, live jazz on a Sunday is exactly the kind of slow, pleasant thing that makes the commute feel worth it — especially on a summer evening when you're not in a rush.

RSVP on the [Gearhouse calendar](https://www.joingearhouse.com/) so they know to expect you. No cover beyond membership.
