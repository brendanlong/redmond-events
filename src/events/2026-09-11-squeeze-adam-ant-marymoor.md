A New Wave triple bill closes out Marymoor Live's season: **Squeeze** ("Tempted," "Black Coffee in Bed") headlines on **Friday, September 11, at 6 p.m.**, with **Adam Ant** ("Stand and Deliver," "Goody Two Shoes") and **Haircut 100** opening. It's a sharp, hooky, unapologetically fun pop-rock lineup — exactly the kind of clever, catchy songwriting that tends to land well with listeners who like their pop with some wit and edge to it.

Marymoor's usual advantages apply: a short walk from downtown Redmond, no parking to deal with, and an outdoor lawn crowd that skews toward people who actually know all three acts' catalogs. It's also a good marker that summer's winding down — this is one of the later shows on the season calendar.

Tickets and set times at [marymoorlive.com](https://www.marymoorlive.com/), via Ticketmaster.
