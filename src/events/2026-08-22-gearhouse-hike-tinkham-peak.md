Another one for the hiking crowd: Gearhouse is leading an intermediate group hike to **Tinkham Peak and Mirror Lake** on **Saturday, August 22**. It's a 5-mile out-and-back with about 2,100 feet of elevation gain — a real workout without being an all-day slog — climbing through forest near Snoqualmie Pass to a peak with views back over the Cascades, with a swim-friendly alpine lake as the payoff.

Like the rest of Gearhouse's I-90 corridor hikes, this is an easy weekend yes: the Mirror Lake trailhead is off I-90 Exit 62, roughly a 45-minute drive east from downtown Redmond, away from the city rather than into it. No weekend Seattle-traffic tax, just a straightforward shot up the pass.

The **time listed above is when to meet the group at the Mirror Lake trailhead** — the carpool leaves Gearhouse's Capitol Hill shop at **8 a.m.** and it's roughly a 75-minute drive up the pass, so plan to be at the trailhead around **9:15**. Driving straight to the trailhead from the Eastside beats backtracking into the city for the shuttle.

This one's listed for Gearhouse's Summit-tier members, so double-check your membership level before RSVPing. Bring trail shoes, plenty of water, and a swimsuit if you want to cool off at the lake before the hike back down.

RSVP on the [Gearhouse calendar](https://www.joingearhouse.com/) to confirm the meet time and carpool details.
