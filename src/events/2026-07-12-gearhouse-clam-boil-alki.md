Gearhouse is doing a **clam boil at Alki Beach** on the evening of Sunday, July 12 — clams, simple sides, and a beachside meal cooked and eaten family-style right on the sand. If you want one of those classic Pacific Northwest summer nights, this is a pretty good version of it: fresh shellfish, a view across Elliott Bay, and a crowd of people who are good at having a good time outside.

Alki is in West Seattle, which from Redmond is about a **35-minute drive** in normal traffic — head through downtown or use the West Seattle Bridge from I-5. Sunday evening is usually fine; the only friction is the West Seattle Bridge backup, which clears out by late afternoon. Worth it for an evening on the beach.

The format is potluck-adjacent: Gearhouse handles the clam boil, you bring a shareable dish. Bring a chair or a blanket — they're setting up right on the beach, not at a table. The food is family-style, the whole thing is casual.

RSVP on the [Gearhouse calendar](https://www.joingearhouse.com/) so they can size the boil. 5:30–8:30 p.m.
