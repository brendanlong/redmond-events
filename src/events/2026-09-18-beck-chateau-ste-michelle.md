**Beck** brings his Ride Lonesome Tour to Chateau Ste. Michelle on **Friday, September 18, at 7 p.m.** For anyone whose taste runs toward the weird, eclectic, genre-hopping end of rock — the guy who made "Loser" and "Where It's At" and then turned around and made a folk-country breakup album with *Sea Change* — this is about as close to a can't-miss as the eastside concert calendar gets this fall. Expect a set that pulls from across a three-decade catalog rather than a single-genre greatest-hits run.

It's the same Chateau Ste. Michelle deal as the rest of the summer series: a 15-20 minute drive to Woodinville, lawn seating, bring-your-own-picnic, and a relaxed wine-country setting that's a nice change of pace from an arena show. September means cooler evenings, so bring a layer.

Tickets and details at [ste-michelle.com](https://www.ste-michelle.com/concerts/summer-concerts).
