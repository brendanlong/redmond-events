**NEEDTOBREATHE** bring their anthemic, radio-friendly rock to Marymoor Park on **Friday, August 21, at 6:30 p.m.** on their Long Surrender Tour, with Drew & Ellie Holcomb opening. They've built a career on big, singalong choruses that land somewhere between rock and mainstream pop — accessible enough for a casual outdoor-lawn crowd, energetic enough to actually get people moving.

It's the same easy Marymoor deal our readers already know: a walk from downtown Redmond, no parking headache, and a lively lawn crowd on a summer Friday evening. Bring a blanket, get there early for a good spot, and expect a set built for the outdoor amphitheater experience.

Tickets and set times at [marymoorlive.com](https://www.marymoorlive.com/), via Ticketmaster.
