**Young the Giant** — the indie-rock band behind "Cough Syrup" and "My Body" — brings its Victory Garden Tour to Marymoor Park on **Saturday, July 25, at 6 p.m.**, with Cold War Kids and Beach Weather opening. It's a genuinely strong indie-rock bill for anyone whose taste runs toward the nerd-rock/indie end of the spectrum rather than straight pop.

Same walkable Marymoor deal as always: 10 minutes from downtown Redmond on foot, no parking stress, and a big outdoor crowd on the park's lawn. This is also the same day as Boyz II Men across the water in Woodinville, so it's a big night for live music on the Eastside if you want to pick your show.

Tickets and set times at [marymoorlive.com](https://www.marymoorlive.com/), via Ticketmaster.
