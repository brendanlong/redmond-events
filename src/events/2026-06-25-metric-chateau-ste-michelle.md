Canadian synth-rock veterans **Metric** headline Chateau Ste. Michelle's outdoor
amphitheater this Thursday, with **Stars** opening — a double bill of moody,
hook-forward indie that's about as easy a "yes" as a summer concert gets if you
lean toward the synthy end of things. Gates are at 6 p.m. for an evening show on
the winery lawn.

The reason this one's worth flagging first: it's *close*. Chateau Ste. Michelle is
in Woodinville, about a **15-minute drive north from downtown Redmond** straight up
Redmond–Woodinville Road — the venue's offsite parking-and-shuttle lot is literally
on that road. For a marquee touring act, that's a remarkably short trip; this is one
of the rare big-name concerts that's practically in our backyard rather than a
fight across the bridges into Seattle.

The format is the Chateau classic: bring a blanket and a low chair, get there early,
and picnic on the lawn with wine before the music starts. It's a relaxed,
grown-up-friendly night out rather than a mosh pit.

This is one of 21 shows in Chateau's summer series, so it's worth keeping an eye on
the [full lineup](https://www.ste-michelle.com/concerts/summer-concerts) — we'll
flag the other standouts as they come up.
