**Kirkland Uncorked** takes over the waterfront at Marina Park **July 17-19**, pairing more than 70 Washington wineries with live music and a craft/maker's market right on the shore of Lake Washington. It's a genuine three-day festival: an all-ages free market to browse even if you skip the wine, plus a ticketed 21+ wine garden ($15-$55) if you want to actually taste your way through the state's wine scene.

This is the kind of event that clears the bar for a drive out to Kirkland easily — good food and drink, live music, a lakefront setting, and it's dog-friendly to boot, so bring the pup along for the market portion. Proceeds support a good cause too: this year's event raises money for local pet organizations.

It's about a 15-20 minute drive from downtown Redmond. Go for an afternoon or make an evening of it — hours run 11 a.m. to 10 p.m. each day. Tickets and the wine garden schedule are at [explorekirkland.com](https://www.explorekirkland.com/events/kirkland-uncorked2/).
