Gen-Z pop-rock favorite **Beabadoobee** brings her **Powerlines Tour** to Climate Pledge Arena on **Thursday, October 29**, in support of her fourth album, *Pylon*, out this September. She's built a catalog that swings between fuzzy '90s-alt guitar hooks and cleaner, radio-ready pop, which makes her a solid pick for anyone whose taste runs from Marina to Death Cab-style indie — she's genuinely both at once, depending on the song.

This is an arena show, so expect full production, a real setlist deep-dive rather than a quick support slot, and a crowd that skews younger and enthusiastic. Climate Pledge Arena is in Seattle Center, about a 25-30 minute drive from downtown Redmond outside of rush hour — doable for a weeknight if you don't mind the return trip, and there's decent transit and rideshare access right at Seattle Center if you'd rather skip parking.

Tickets are on sale now; given the album drops right before the tour, expect setlists leaning heavily on new material alongside earlier favorites. Details and tickets at [climatepledgearena.com](https://www.climatepledgearena.com/).
