Two Marymoor shows in one week: on **Sunday, July 12 at 7 p.m.**, the **Marcus King Band** brings its Grammy-nominated blend of Southern rock, blues, and soul to the outdoor stage, with Penelope Road opening. King is one of the more talked-about young guitarists in roots music right now — think searing, Allman-Brothers-style guitar work paired with a genuinely soulful voice — and a Sunday evening slot means you're not sacrificing a work night to see him.

Same easy math as always for a Marymoor show: it's a walk or quick bike ride from downtown Redmond, so there's no drive to plan around and no need to leave before the encore to beat traffic.

Tickets and the full lineup are at [marymoorlive.com](https://www.marymoorlive.com/), sold via Ticketmaster.
