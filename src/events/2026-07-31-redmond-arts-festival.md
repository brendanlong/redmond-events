**Redmond Arts Festival** takes over Redmond Town Center for a three-day outdoor art show, **July 31 - August 2**: more than 60 juried artist booths, a community art project, kids' art activities, and live entertainment running continuously in Center Court all three days — the lineup ranges from blues and soft rock to jazz, a Chinese choir, and Thai dance, so there's genuinely something different happening every hour you wander by.

It's a completely walkable one for downtown Redmond residents — the whole thing is at Redmond Town Center, so no driving or parking to think about. Good for a lazy weekend afternoon: browse the art, grab food from RTC's restaurants, and catch whatever's playing on the Center Court stage.

Hours are **Friday and Saturday, noon-8 p.m., and Sunday, noon-6 p.m.** Free admission. Details at [valaeastside.org/redmondartsfestival](https://www.valaeastside.org/redmondartsfestival).
