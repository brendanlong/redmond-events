Gearhouse's **Paddle Club** is back out on **Wednesday, August 12**, this time at **Green Lake** for a post-work sunset paddle. Green Lake is about as forgiving a paddling spot as exists in the city — flat, calm, and shallow enough that it's genuinely beginner-friendly, with a mile-plus of shoreline to explore if you want to make a real loop of it rather than just floating around.

Like their Magnuson Park outing earlier in the month, this is a weeknight trip into Seattle — call it a 25-30 minute drive from downtown Redmond depending on the route and time of day. The tradeoff is the same: warm evening light on the water and a crew to paddle with, in exchange for a weekday crossing. Boards are typically available for members who don't want to haul their own, per the RSVP details.

RSVP on the [Gearhouse calendar](https://www.joingearhouse.com/) for the exact meet spot and whether to bring your own board. Bring a swimsuit, a towel, and a dry bag for your phone.
