The Redmond Saturday Market is back at Redmond Town Center this Saturday, and if
you're anywhere near downtown it's an easy one to fold into your morning — it's
about a 15-minute walk from the heart of downtown Redmond, right where the Sammamish
River Trail meets the shops.

Expect the usual summer-market lineup: Washington-grown produce, a row of food
vendors, local makers, and live buskers drifting between the stalls. It runs
9am–3pm, so there's no rush; wander down mid-morning, grab coffee and a pastry,
and do a loop. It's free to walk through, and dog-friendly if you bring a leash.

For our crowd this is a low-stakes, high-charm outing — the kind of walkable,
no-planning-required thing that's worth knowing is happening even on a quiet
weekend. If you've been meaning to try more of the local food vendors, this is the
spot to graze.
