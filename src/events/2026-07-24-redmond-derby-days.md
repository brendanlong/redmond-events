Redmond's signature summer celebration is back: **Derby Days** returns **July 24-25** with two days of parades, live music, food vendors, a craft fair, and a Kids' Cove, all centered on downtown Redmond and the Municipal Campus — as walkable as an event gets for this newsletter. Live music runs on the Main Stage (brought to you by Microsoft) both days, and Saturday night wraps up with a **drone light show** over the Redmond sky, courtesy of Amazon — a nice, low-key alternative to fireworks that's become the event's signature closer.

This is the kind of hometown festival that's worth clearing a weekend for even if you're not a parade person: the parade itself, food trucks, local vendors, and a genuinely festive, family-friendly atmosphere across the whole downtown core. If you've lived in Redmond a while, this is the one event that reliably gets the whole neighborhood out.

Full schedule, parade route, and road closures are posted at [redmond.gov/1138/Derby-Days](https://www.redmond.gov/1138/Derby-Days) as the date approaches.
